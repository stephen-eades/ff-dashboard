This is your league.

Every ESPN fantasy league is assigned a league ID upon creation.

This site uses that ID to gather, analyze, and display your data so you can learn more about your league. View your league's current standings, see aggregated totals from past seasons, or even track trades and league trends.

Curious? Go ahead, click next.

Find your ID.

When you visit ESPN.com and go to your league home page, you'll be able to see your league ID in the URL. You can see how it will appear in the example below:

www.espn.com/...leagueId=93752695...

Simply find this number and copy it down. You can then use this number for the final step! Click next once you have your league ID ready.

Enter your ID

Great, we're almost there. Just enter your league ID in the field below and click submit. You'll then be brought to a dashboard made just for your league!
